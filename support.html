Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I make a reservation?

    To make a reservation, log in to your account, select your departure and destination, choose your travel dates, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the booking process.

  • Can I modify my reservation?

    Yes, you can modify your reservation by logging in to your account and navigating to the "My Reservations" section. Follow the prompts to make changes to your booking.

  • What payment methods are accepted?

    We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and other major payment methods. Make sure to check the payment options during the booking process.

  • Is Wi-Fi available on flights?

    Wi-Fi is available on select flights. You can check the availability and pricing during the booking process or by contacting our customer support.

  • How can I request a refund?

    If you need to request a refund, please contact our customer support team. Refund policies may vary based on the type of ticket and the time of cancellation.

  • Are pets allowed on flights?

    Yes, pets are allowed on certain flights. Please review our pet policy and contact our customer support for more information on traveling with pets.
